Mutator: Minesweeper

Groups of Widow Mines and Spider Mines are buried throughout the battlefield.

Difficulty points6
Available in customYes
Rollable in Chaos StudiosNo

Detailed Mutator Information

At the start of the game, a total of 25 patches of Widow Mines and Spider Mines will be created. Each patch will contain 5 Widow Mines and 7 Spider Mines. The patches cannot be placed within 30 range of player start locations or within 5 range of resource patches or within 8 range of each other.

Widow Mines deal direct target damage 250 (+35 vs shields) and splash damage of 80 (+40 vs shields). They have 180HP and will trigger from 5 range.

Spider Mines deal a base damage of 250 within range 1.7, 50% of base damage within range 2.25, and 25% of base damage within range 3. They have 25HP and will trigger from 4 range.

Since both, Spider Mines and Widow Mines do not attack structures, commanders with movable static defense are great at efficiently clearing mines.

Mines will trigger on Aurana's transport on Malwarfare, Terrazine Harvesters on Mist Opportunities, Artifact Trucks on Cradle of Death and the Balius on Part and Parcel.

Additionally, there are some other map-specific limitations:

  • Chain of Ascension: Patches will not be placed within 15 range of Ji'Nara.
  • Lock & Load: Patches will not be placed within 8 range of the Celestial Locks.

Commander-specific Tips

  • Kerrigan: 25 points into the Immobilization Wave mastery will allow Kerrigan to 1-shot Widow Mines.
  • Kerrigan: Folly of Man Fury stacks can hit mines without detection.
  • Stetmann: Getting the Hardened Egonergy Shield upgrade for Zerglings will allow them to clear mines efficiently without taking heavy losses.

Mutator Interactions

Alien IncubationMines spawn Broodlings when they are destroyed.
AvengerMines can give out Avenger stacks, but cannot receive them.
BarrierMines get the Barrier buff.
Black DeathMines can carry Plague.
Concussive AttacksMines apply the Concussive Attacks debuff.
Evasive ManeuversMines do not get the Evasive Maneuvers buff.
Fatal AttractionMines trigger Fatal Attraction when they are destroyed.
FearMines can apply the Fear effect.
FireworksMines do not launch Fireworks when they are destroyed.
Gift ExchangeMines can capture gifts.
Hardened WillMines cause Hardened Will to activate.
Just DieMines respawn when they are destroyed.
Laser DrillMines provide vision for the Laser Drill.
Long RangeMines do not get the Long Range buff.
Naughty ListMine kills increase the Naughty List count.
PolarityWidow Mines and Spider Mines all have Polarity applied to them.
Power OverwhelmingWidow mines can cast abilities. Spider mines cannot cast abilities.
Scorched EarthMines set the ground on fire when they die.
Self DestructionMines set the ground on fire when they are destroyed.
Speed FreaksSpider mines do not move faster with Speed Freaks.
TransmutationWidow Mines Transmute after killing a unit.
Void ReanimatorsVoid Reanimators resurrect Widow Mines but not Spider Mines. Widow Mines will move towards player bases after resurrection.
Walking InfestedMines spawned Infested when they are destroyed.
